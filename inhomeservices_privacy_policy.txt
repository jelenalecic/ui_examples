InHomeServices Privacy Policy
-------------------------------------

InHomeServices allows services to register its service and offer it to customers, in a form
of a yellow pages app. Service enters its type, address, price-list and location. 
Customers find services by entering their location and category.
Everything is free, for both customer and service.

Does this app use any personal information?
It can take user's location but it's not mandatory. User can enter its location manually.

Does this app store any personal information?
It stores data that service enters in registration process only on server side.
App does not store anything locally. 

What User Data We Collect?
None.
